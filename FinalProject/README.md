# CapyBerry 
A mobile puzzle game app that is themed around a Capybara gathering fruits through matching the appropriate orbs. The gameplay is inspired by Puzzles & Dragons, which is a puzzle click and drag matching RPG game. The aim is to get as many orb matches in as possible and achieve a high score before the timer runs out. 

## Quick Start
Upon launch, the user can begin playing by selecting the Start Game button. They will then redirected to the Gameplay screen, where the core feature of the app is. 

## Game Mechanics
Users can drag individual orbs around the board, which will switch adjacent orbs in the direction that the dragged orb came from.

Users have a limited time to perform a drag, which is indicated by a stamina circle above their tap.

Extra points are earned for each additional orb that is matched in a group. 

An exponential amount of points are given based on the amount of orb matches that a player makes from one dragging move. 

A timer of 2 minutes will end the game once it runs out.

## Visual Mechanics
The combo count pop-up will change colors after a combo count of 3x. 

After a combo count of 7x, pop-ups will have a rainbow effect and animation.

The capybara avatar will perform a random animation every 3x combo multiple.
